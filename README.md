# Watsco (watsco)

Watsco, Inc. is the world's largest distributor of air conditioning, heating, and refrigeration (HVAC/R) equipment and related products. Operating through subsidiaries including Carrier Enterprise, Baker Distributing, Gemaire Distributors, and East Coast Metal Distributors, Watsco serves contractors and dealers across more than 670 locations in the US, Canada, Mexico, and the Caribbean. The company has undergone significant digital transformation, running a unified B2B e-commerce platform powered by Magento Commerce and a centralized PIM system covering approximately 900,000 SKUs.
